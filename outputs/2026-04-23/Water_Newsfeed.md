## 2. Urban Water Supply and Use

### New South Wales - Urban Water Supply and Use

**WaterNSW dam safety project wins top AWA award** WaterNSW has announced that "[An automated high-tech dam safety surveillance system installed at a critical Sydney water supply storage has won WaterNSW a major infrastructure award at the Australian Water Association (AWA) annual NSW awards.]". According to WaterNSW, "[The WaterNSW Prospect Automated Surveying System project won the NSW Infrastructure Innovation award for implementing an advanced real-time monitoring network at Prospect Reservoir, which stores water from Warragamba Dam, and is located approximately 34km west of Sydney.]". WaterNSW's media release (18 MARCH 2026) *(Source: WaterNSW)*

*https://www.waternsw.com.au/community-news/media-releases/2026/waternsw-dam-safety-project-wins-top-awa-award*
