Sunwater has advised that "the Queensland Water Licensing Framework 2026 has been revised to streamline licence applications and introduce new compliance obligations for large-scale irrigators across the Burdekin-Haughton catchment."

According to Sunwater, "applicants will now be able to lodge and track licence applications through the Sunwater Customer Portal, reducing processing times by an estimated 40 percent."

Key changes include:

  • a single digital application form replacing three legacy paper forms;
  • quarterly compliance reporting for irrigators taking more than 1,000 ML/year; and
  • clearer penalties for unmetered water take under the Water Act 2000 (Qld).

The revised framework takes effect on 1 May 2026. Download the full framework (PDF) for detailed guidance.
