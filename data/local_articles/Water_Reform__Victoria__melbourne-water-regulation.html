Melbourne Water Regulation 2026 Takes Effect

18 April 2026

Melbourne Water has announced that "the Melbourne Water Regulation 2026 (Victoria) has commenced, introducing updated compliance obligations for all licensed water users operating within the Port Phillip and Westernport region."

According to Melbourne Water, "the new Regulation consolidates and modernises three earlier instruments and introduces mandatory quarterly reporting for major urban water authorities."

The Regulation:

  • requires all licensed water users to submit quarterly water take declarations;
  • introduces a tiered penalty framework for non-compliance with cease-to-take directions; and
  • clarifies the obligations of water corporations under the Water Act 1989 (Vic).

The Regulation commenced on 15 April 2026 and will be reviewed after three years of operation.
